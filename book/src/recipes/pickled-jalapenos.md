# Pickled Jalapenos

{{#include cook-mode.md}}

## Ingredients
* 7 green jalapenos
* 1 red jalapeno
* couple of pieces of white onion (optional)
* couple of peeled and smashed cloves of garlic
* 1 cup water
* 1 cup white vinegar
* 1 Tablespoon salt

## Instructions
* Slice peppers and put into mason jar
* Bring water and vinegar to a boil
* Add salt
* Pour into mason jar
